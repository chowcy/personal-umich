About Me

Hello there! I am currently a senior at the University of Michigan and will be graduating in May with a Bachelor of Science in German. I know - it's kind of a weird degree to have since most German majors get a Bachelor of Arts, but I love science too much to not get a degree in it! My philosophy during my four years at U of M has always been to learn as much as possible. As a result, I have taken courses or attended workshops in a variety of different fields. My intellectual pursuits include biology, mathematics, public health, foreign languages and translation, education, psychology, nutritional science, statistics, and more recently, programming! Right now I'm teaching myself some basic web development and this website is a little independent project I'm working on :)

In late August, I will be heading to Germany to teach English to German students for 10 months as part of the Fulbright English Teaching Assistantship Grant Program. I am currently looking for an internship or job for the summer of 2015 in Ann Arbor. Fields: programming, translation, or teaching, but in all honesty, I would be happy to work for any employer that will utilize my skillset, challenge me, and treat both its employees and customers with the respect they deserve, regardless of the field.
